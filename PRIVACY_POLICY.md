# PocketDeskies Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026
_Last updated: April 3, 2026_
**Version:** 1.0.0

PocketDeskies ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and maintained by SudarshanTechLabs. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Information We Collect

**PocketDeskies does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

No account or sign-in is required. All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

All functionality runs locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.

## Storage and Retention

- App preferences are stored locally on your device.
- No SudarshanTechLabs server stores any data.

## Data Security

- Local data is protected by Android's built-in storage encryption.

## Permissions Used

Only the minimum permissions required for core functionality are requested. No permissions that access personal data are used.

## Your Rights and Controls

- Clear all app data via Android Settings → Apps → PocketDeskies → Clear Data.
- Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored preferences.

## Children's Privacy

PocketDeskies is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
